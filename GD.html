Brief
Use Case 1.1

Log in

The user enters their username and password to start the game.


Casual
Use Case 1.2

Find pokemon

The player uses the W, A, S, D or arrow keys to move the character around the "game world".
While the player is moving around the game world they have a chance to find a pokemon in certain sections of the map where there is tall grass.
When a pokemon is found by the player, a fight initiates.

If the player's pokemon is dead then they are forced to retreat from battle if a pokemon is found.


Fully Dressed
Use Case 1.3

Battle

Scenario 1 (Player attacks enemy pokemon)

  • Pre-conditions: The user must find a Pokémon in the tall grass.
    1. The player and the Pokémon gets teleported to another location where the battle takes place.
    2. A small GUI with the player's options appears.
    3. The player chooses to attack the enemy pokemon.
    4. The enemy pokemon takes a random amount of damage.
    5. The player must now wait for the enemy pokemon to attack before they can attack again.
    6. The enemy pokemon attacks.
    7. The player's pokemon takes a random amount of damage.
    8. This process continues until one pokemon's health points reach zero.
    9. The pokemon with zero health points dies.
    10. The dead pokemon is removed from the scene.
    11. A screen with the name of the victor appears.
