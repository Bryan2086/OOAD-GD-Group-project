Brief
Use Case 1.1

Log in

The user enters their username and password to start the game.


Casual
Use Case 1.2

Find pokemon

The player uses the W, A, S, D or arrow keys to move the character around the "game world".
While the player is moving around the game world they have a chance to find a pokemon in certain sections of the map where there is tall grass.
When a pokemon is found by the player, a fight initiates.

If the player's pokemon is dead then they are forced to retreat from battle if a pokemon is found.


Fully Dressed
Use Case 1.3

Title: player battles pokemon in tall grass

Actor : Player

Pre-Condition

  • The player must be in the tall grass.

    • Post-Condition

  • The player is teleported back to the original scene.

    • Main Success Scenario

    1. The player takes a step and encounters a pokemon.
    2. The player and pokemon are teleported to another scene where turn based combat takes place.
    3. The system displays a gui with the player's options.
    4. The player chooses the attack option and the enemy pokemon takes damage.
    5. The enemy's turn begins and the enemy pokemon attacks
    6. The player's pokemon takes damage.
    7. Repeat steps 4-6 until one pokemon's hp reaches 0.
    8. A message is displayed declaring the winner.

      9. Extensions

      4a 1. The player chooses to use a health potion.
      4a 2. The player's pokemon is healed by 30 hp.
      4a 3. The health potion is removed from the player's inventory.

      4b 1. The player chooses the retreat option.
